Stomach Surgery

Dr. Jenit Gandhi specializes in advanced stomach surgeries, offering minimally invasive techniques for conditions such as gastric ulcers, tumors, and reflux disease.

Esophagus Surgery

Dr. Jenit Gandhi excels in performing esophagus surgeries, addressing conditions such as esophageal cancer, achalasia, and severe GERD. Using state-of-the-art laparoscopic techniques, he ensures precise interventions with minimal recovery time and optimal patient outcomes.

Pancreas Surgery

Dr. Jenit Gandhi provides expert care in pancreas surgery, treating conditions like pancreatic cancer, cysts, and chronic pancreatitis. His proficiency in laparoscopic and open surgical techniques ensures comprehensive care tailored to each patient's needs, promoting better health and faster recovery.

Billiary Surgery

Dr. Jenit Gandhi specializes in biliary surgery, addressing issues such as gallstones, bile duct obstructions, and biliary tract cancers. Utilizing advanced laparoscopic methods, he delivers precise and effective treatments, ensuring improved patient outcomes and quicker recovery times.

Gall Bladder Surgery

Dr. Jenit Gandhi is an expert in gall bladder surgery, effectively treating conditions such as gallstones and cholecystitis. He employs minimally invasive laparoscopic techniques to ensure a safer procedure with reduced recovery time and minimal discomfort for his patients.

Colon Ano-Rectal Surgery

Dr. Jenit Gandhi is highly skilled in colon and ano-rectal surgery, treating conditions such as colorectal cancer, hemorrhoids, and anal fissures. His expertise in laparoscopic and open surgical techniques ensures precise interventions, promoting better outcomes and faster recovery.

How we work?

01. Consultation
Your journey begins with a thorough consultation, where Dr. Gandhi listens intently to understand your health concerns, providing a compassionate space for you to discuss your symptoms and fears.
02. Pre Surgery Care
Before considering surgery, we explore all medication-based treatments, aiming for the least invasive options to manage or alleviate your condition, ensuring a personalized care plan.
03. Surgery
If surgery is necessary, Dr. Gandhi utilizes the latest minimally invasive techniques, emphasizing precision, safety, and the best possible outcomes with reduced recovery times.
04. Post Surgery Care
Post-operative care is paramount in our practice, focusing on comprehensive support, pain management, and follow-up consultations to ensure a smooth and speedy recovery.
