Chronic Pancreatitis

Chronic pancreatitis is a long-lasting inflammation of the pancreas that can significantly impact quality of life. When conservative treatments like medication and dietary changes fail to provide relief, surgery may be necessary to alleviate symptoms, prevent complications, and improve overall health. If you are looking for the best chronic pancreatitis treatment doctors in Ahmedabad, it is crucial to consult an experienced professional.

When is Surgery Needed?

Surgery for chronic pancreatitis is generally considered when:

  • Severe Pain: Persistent, debilitating pain that is not manageable with medication. Consulting a pancreas specialist in Ahmedabad can help manage severe cases.
  • Complications: Development of complications such as pseudocysts, bile duct obstruction, or pancreatic cancer. A pancreas specialist in Ahmedabad may recommend surgery in these situations.
  • Pancreatic Dysfunction: Severe cases where the pancreas loses its ability to function properly, affecting digestion and blood sugar levels. The best chronic pancreatitis treatment doctors in Ahmedabad can offer advanced solutions in these cases.
  • Failed Conservative Treatments: When other treatments, including medications, enzyme supplements, and lifestyle changes, do not provide sufficient relief. Itâ€™s essential to consider chronic pancreatitis treatment in Ahmedabad under expert guidance.

Types of Surgery

There are several surgical options available for chronic pancreatitis, depending on the severity of the condition and the specific needs of the patient:

1. Pancreatic Resection:
  • Involves the removal of the diseased part of the pancreas. Types of resection include Whipple procedure (pancreatoduodenectomy), distal pancreatectomy, and total pancreatectomy. The best chronic pancreatitis treatment doctors in Ahmedabad will recommend the most suitable option.
2. Drainage Procedures:
  • For patients with dilated pancreatic ducts, procedures like Puestow procedure (lateral pancreaticojejunostomy) are performed to drain the pancreatic ducts and relieve pain. For such cases, chronic pancreatitis treatment in Ahmedabad offers advanced drainage techniques.
3. Pancreaticojejunostomy:
  • A procedure where the pancreatic duct is connected to the jejunum (a part of the small intestine) to allow better drainage and relieve pressure.
4. Total Pancreatectomy with Islet Autotransplantation (TPIAT):
  • In cases where the entire pancreas needs to be removed, this procedure is combined with islet cell transplantation to help prevent diabetes.

What to Expect Before Surgery

Before surgery, thorough diagnostic evaluations, including imaging tests and laboratory studies, will be conducted to assess the extent of pancreatic damage and plan the most appropriate surgical approach. Dr. Jenit Gandhi, one of the best chronic pancreatitis treatment doctors in Ahmedabad, will discuss the benefits and risks associated with each surgical option and tailor the treatment plan to the patient's specific condition.

Recovery and Aftercare

Recovery from chronic pancreatitis surgery varies based on the type of surgery performed. Generally, patients can expect:

  • Hospital Stay: A stay of several days to a week in the hospital for monitoring and initial recovery.
  • Pain Management: Post-operative pain management with medications and support.
  • Dietary Changes: Guidance on diet modifications to support healing and improve digestion.
  • Follow-Up Care: Regular follow-up visits to monitor recovery, manage any complications, and assess pancreatic function.
